Social referrals and word of mouth are some of the best sources of business for service providers and professionals (pros) like doctors, lawyers, plumbers etc. Yet, service providers are mostly passive in the process, besides providing referral-worthy service. Imagine getting notified when anyone in your social neighborhood (friends and friends of friends) mentions keywords related to your service. Also imagine being able to connect with such potential customers at the click of a button. Pros Social Leads is an app that: a) finds potential customers on social media who either mention your keywords in their updates, or have them in their profiles; b) ranks the leads found based on social proximity, intelligent relevance to keywords, and optional physical location; c) and allows you to contact specific leads with the click of a button.

Start using the app by simply selecting the social networks you want to check, and enter the keywords or keyword phrases to search for. The app shows a rank ordered list of matching leads. This list is smartly ranked based on a) whether the search terms are in lead-users' profile or in their updates, b) as well as the social proximity of the lead-user to the app-user. Note that a Facebook 'friend' is closer socially compared to a 'friend of a friend'. The app then allows the app-user to select specific users to contact. The application user can type in a custom message or simply choose one of the previously saved messages to send to the selected leads found. Every new message text is saved for future use, if desired.

In the full version of the app, there is no limit on the number of searches ever. It also allows realtime tracking of keywords configured, so that updates are emailed to the app-user at configured intervals with new leads found. Any app-user can also use their credentials on the app's website for the same app functionality.
